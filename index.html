History

The California drought is said to be the worst drought in 1200 years.

We are now in severe drought conditions with 77% of the state in an extreme drought and the other 33% considered to be in an exceptional drought according to the National Drought Mitigation Center.

  • Los Angeles: 6.08 inches (15.44 cm); 41 percent of average
  • Sacramento: 10.35 in (26.29 cm); 51 percent of average
  • San Francisco: 12.54 in (31.85 cm); 53 percent of average
  • San Diego: 5.06 in (12.85 cm); 49 percent of average
  • Fresno: 4.81 in (12.22 cm); 42 percent of average
